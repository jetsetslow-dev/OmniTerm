# Commercial Use

OmniTerm source is available for personal, educational, research, evaluation,
and other noncommercial use under the PolyForm Noncommercial License 1.0.0.

Commercial use requires written permission from the copyright holder.

Examples of commercial use that require permission:

- Publishing OmniTerm or a fork to an app store for payment.
- Selling signed APKs, AABs, managed builds, or paid update channels.
- Bundling OmniTerm into a paid product or service.
- Charging for hosted, managed, supported, or white-labeled OmniTerm builds.
- Monetizing forks through subscriptions, unlocks, ads, sponsorship-only access,
  or paid binaries.

Allowed without a commercial license:

- Building OmniTerm from source for your own devices.
- Modifying OmniTerm for personal or internal noncommercial use.
- Filing issues, sending patches, and sharing noncommercial improvements.
- Evaluating the code to decide whether to request a commercial license.

The Play Store build has the same app features as the source-available build.
The Play Store build is free for one saved host; the one-time Play Store
purchase removes host/profile limits and pays for signed distribution,
automatic updates, and ongoing maintenance convenience.
